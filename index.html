About Us

A Home For Every Paw is an awareness campaign that raises the issues of cruelty towards companion dogs and supports adoption of rescue dogs. We aim to educate people about adopting and encourage supporting animal welfare organisations.

What is the issue?

Its a failed system... and change starts with us.
Neglect and abuse
Total of 56,969 cruelty compaints of dogs were investigated in 2023-2024 in Australia.
Unethical dog farms
Dog farms have unethical conditions for the dogs that are overcrowded and starving.
Over-flooding shelters
Many dogs are killed in council pounds every year and aren't given a chance to find a loving home.
Euthanasia Practices
Euthanasia of impounded pets but only kill them but also risk the mental health of vet staff.

By raising awareness and encouraging adoption it reduces animal cruelty.
